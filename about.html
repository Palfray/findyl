About findyl

A UK vinyl discovery platform helping collectors find records, check pricing, and support independent record stores.

Our story

findyl was born from a simple frustration: finding vinyl records online shouldn't be complicated. Whether you're hunting for a rare pressing of a classic album or discovering new artists, we believe vinyl collectors deserve better tools.

Our goal is to make vinyl discovery easier. Instead of visiting dozens of websites to check availability or find pricing, we're building a place where vinyl enthusiasts can search records and see what's available. From online retailers to the Discogs marketplace, findyl brings information together in one place.

Based in the UK, we're passionate about vinyl culture and supporting the independent record stores that make our music communities special. We've created a directory of over 285 local record shops across the country, making it easier to discover and support your local stores.

What we do

Search vinyl records

Search vinyl releases by artist or album. Our goal is to help you find records across different sources, from new releases to classic albums.

Find pricing information

See retail prices from UK stores like POPSTORE and marketplace pricing from Discogs. Our goal is to help you make informed buying decisions.

Discover local record stores

Browse our directory of 285 independent UK record shops. Support local businesses and experience the joy of crate digging in person.

Connect with UK retailers

We work with online retailers like POPSTORE and connect you with reputable marketplaces. Our goal is to help you find records from trusted sources.

Why findyl is different

UK-focused platform

Built specifically for UK vinyl collectors. We focus on UK retailers, UK pricing in pounds, and UK independent record stores.

Album-focused search

We search albums only—no singles or EPs cluttering your results. Our goal is to help you find what you're looking for faster with intelligent filtering.

Pricing transparency

See retail prices and used marketplace pricing when available. Our goal is to help you make informed decisions about whether to buy new or hunt for a bargain.

Support independent stores

We champion UK independent record shops. Every search connects you with local stores, helping preserve vinyl culture in your community.
