About findyl

The UK's vinyl price comparison platform. Search any artist, compare prices across retailers, and find the best deal before you buy.

What findyl does

findyl is a price comparison tool for vinyl records. You search for an artist or album, and we show you prices from UK retailers and marketplaces — new and used — so you can find the best deal without visiting half a dozen different websites.

Every album page pulls pricing from multiple sources and shows them side by side. We also show you the full discography for every artist, so you can browse what's available and check prices across their entire catalogue.

Who it's for

findyl is for anyone who buys vinyl in the UK and wants to compare prices before they buy. Whether you're six months into collecting or you've been digging through crates for decades, the value is the same: find the record you want at the best price, from a retailer you can trust.

We're not a catalogue or a marketplace. We don't sell records. We point you to the retailers who do — and we try to make sure you're seeing all your options in one place.

Independent retailers

Independent record shops account for over a third of all vinyl sold in the UK. They're a vital part of the ecosystem, and findyl is designed to surface them alongside the bigger names. Our store directory lists over 285 independent shops across the UK, and we're always working to bring more independent online retailers into our price comparison results.
