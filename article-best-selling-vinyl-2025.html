The 20 Best Selling Vinyl Records of 2025

By findyl · 17 February 2026 · 5 min read

UK vinyl sales hit 7.6 million units in 2025 — the 18th consecutive year of growth for the format. It was a year defined by huge new releases, blockbuster reissues, and the unstoppable force of Taylor Swift, who claimed the top spot for a fourth year running. Here are the 20 best selling vinyl albums of the year, based on Official Charts Company data.

What does this tell us?

A few things stand out. Taylor Swift's dominance is now undeniable — four consecutive years at the top of the vinyl chart, and The Life of a Showgirl shifted 126,000 copies in its first week alone, the fastest-selling vinyl album this century. Sam Fender's People Watching is the feel-good story of the year for British music, breaking the record for the biggest opening week by a British artist on vinyl.

Oasis were everywhere, occupying three slots in the top 20 off the back of their reunion tour and a wave of anniversary reissues. And Rumours by Fleetwood Mac remains one of the most enduring records in history — this is its ninth consecutive year in the UK vinyl year-end top 10.

The presence of Pulp, Wolf Alice, Wet Leg and Fontaines D.C. is encouraging for anyone who cares about independent-minded British music having a place in the mainstream. 2025 was a good year for vinyl.
